# Quick Task 260520-n5i: Prevent Duplicate re:Fee Energy Rental

## Goal

Fix the production bug where a fixed `REFEE_FIXED_ENERGY_ORDER_AMOUNT=65000`
order can be repeated when re:Fee delegates `64,999` available energy even
though the eventual TRC20 transfer only needs less energy.

Observed production facts:

- Estimated energy before rental: `72,321`.
- Fixed order amount: `65,000`.
- First re:Fee order left `64,999` available energy on-chain.
- Actual successful USDT transfer used `64,285` energy and `345` bandwidth.
- The first attempt stopped because the provider post-check compared
  `64,999 < 65,000`; the next 120-second scan rented another `65,000`.
